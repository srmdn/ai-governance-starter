# Go-To-Market AI Risk Checklist

Run this before publishing AI-assisted marketing/sales content or claims.

## Claims and Evidence

- [ ] every factual claim has a source
- [ ] competitor comparisons are current and verifiable
- [ ] benchmark statements include methodology and scope

## Legal and Trust

- [ ] no deceptive or unverifiable performance claims
- [ ] no fabricated customer logos/testimonials
- [ ] required disclaimers are present

## Messaging Quality

- [ ] target audience and use case are clearly stated
- [ ] limitations are explicitly communicated
- [ ] wording avoids false certainty

## Operational Readiness

- [ ] support team has accurate product behavior notes
- [ ] escalation path exists for incorrect claims in market
- [ ] rollback plan exists for published mistakes

## Final Gate

- [ ] human reviewer signs off before publication
